User Story:
Tasmin is a frequent viewer and binge watcher of many movies and shows. It is her favourite thing to do in her free time especially after a long shift. Due to this she frequently falls asleep in the middle of watching a show so a feature where inactivity is detected and automatically pauses the show and can give her a brief recap on the previous episode.
She mainly watches the media on her laptop late at night but while watching on a weekend the inactivity feature could get annoying so being able to disable it early through a setting in either the accessibility options or when starting a show is a necessitate. 

Testing Criteria One:
As a frequent user I want to be able to see all the shows I am watching I also want a regularly update suggestion of what to watch next when I finish a series. 
Testing Criteria Two:
As a user who regularly falls asleep during content I want an auto pause, though this feature should also be able to be disabled. It would also be nice if I can get a recap on the last episode watched


Acceptance Criteria One:
On the main landing page the first category is continue watching, the second is the users list of shows they have marked. After this it is organised by category according to what we predict the user will most enjoy based of promotions and what they already consume.
Acceptance Criteria Two:
With regards to the auto pause feature after a set amount of inactivity the show will automatically stop and bring up a screen of; continue watching, continue without interruption and exit. Continue without interruption is how we are addressing the fact the feature needs to be disabled so it will only ping once per a show.
Acceptance Criteria Three:
Moving onto the recap this is similar to profile two so we can reuse the same feature to address both their concerns within the media preview page with the automatic recap text.
