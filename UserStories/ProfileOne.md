User Story 
Ruben Washington is a young man  who spends  most of his time gaming, watching shows and movies on his pc. He lives whit his family and enjoys watching movies with them too on occasion. Ruben has had glasses since he was young and requires easy to read and high contrast titles. He wants everything to be easy to read when browsing. All titles should be easily visible and high contrast from the background making them standout. 

Testing Criteria One:
As a user with visual impairments I want content to be easily readable for media titles and navigation options

Testing Criteria Two:
As a first time user I want an intuitive and easy to navigate site so that I can quickly and easily sign up and get to watching content

Testing Criteria Three :
As a budget conscious user I want the service to be cheap and easy to cancel preferably by either a free ad supported plan or a free trial so I can easily see what content is available

Acceptance Criteria One:
As the user is visually impaired we accommodated for this by checking our colour schemes contrast against WCAG Standards all the dark themes  align with AAA standard. We also make sure that all navigation options fit within this high contrast and obvious placing.

Acceptance Criteria Two:
With the first time users the signup form is easy to navigate with high contrast boxes that guide the user through the form on what they need to type. When they have successfully signed up they will land on the main page which will direct them with suggested/ promoted content and easy to navigate boxes.

Acceptance Criteria Three:
As a the user is budget conscious we implemented a 30 day free trial evident in the sign up form if the user wants to cancel their free trial they should be able to do so from the accounts page though this will not be implemented into the frameworks.
