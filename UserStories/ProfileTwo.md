User Story
William Johnson is a returning user who has hearing problems. He lives alone and watches his shows and movies on his TV. (Falkowski-Gilski and Uhl, 2020) Due to his partial deafness William needs subtitles that are highly customisable to be able to consume content easily, he does occasionally have his grandkids over so having multiple users would be nice so that a kid friendly profile can be setup. 

Testing Criteria One:
As a user with hearing difficulties I want to be able to easily customise my subtitles so I can easily consume my content.
Testing Criteria Two:
As a returning user I want the content I may have watched a month or two ago to still be available with my location saved. As well as a recap blurb to remind me of the past few episodes.

Acceptance Criteria One:
With needing highly customisable subtitles we can look at the video player framework as within that you can see  we have options for Text colour, background colour and background opacity.
Acceptance Criteria Two:
With wanting to see what he was watching we have the myList section which allows users to store long term shows they are interested in and the first carousel is the continue watching segment for the shows they have been watching. Within the media preview page along with the show and episode blurb we can incorporate a quick recap of the season so far.
